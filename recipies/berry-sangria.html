Berry Sangria

Refreshing, tasty cocktail

Packed with juice, brandy, fresh fruit and wine

Ingredients

  • ½ cup grapefruit juice
  • 3 tbsps white sugar
  • 1 bottle dry Rosé
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • ½ cup fresh blackberries
  • ¼ cup brandy
  • 1 ½ cups club soda
  • 3 cups ice

Directions

Step 1

Stir together grapefruit juice and sugar in a pitcher until sugar dissolves. Add wine, raspberries, blackberries, lime slices, and brandy.

Step 2

Cover and chill until ready to serve, 1 to 24 hours.

Step 3

Gently stir in club soda before serving over ice.
